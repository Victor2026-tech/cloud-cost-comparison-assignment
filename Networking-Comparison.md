Networking Cost Comparison

Inter-zone data transfer occurs when resources communicate across different availability zones.

AWS:

Approximate inter-zone transfer cost: $0.01 per GB

Azure:

Approximate inter-zone transfer cost: $0.01 to $0.02 per GB

Example:
For 500 GB of inter-zone traffic:

AWS: Approximately $5
Azure: Approximately $5–$10

Proper application architecture can reduce unnecessary networking charges.
